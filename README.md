# Dr. Muhammad Reshaeel

**Mechanical Engineering researcher | Physics-informed machine learning | HVAC energy systems | Fault detection and diagnostics**

I am a Mechanical Engineering researcher based in Abu Dhabi, working at the intersection of thermodynamics, energy systems, and data-driven modelling. My research focuses on physics-informed machine learning, variable refrigerant flow cooling systems, energy prediction, and interpretable fault detection for smart and sustainable buildings.

## Research Focus

- Physics-informed machine and deep learning for energy system modelling
- Energy prediction and grey-box modelling for HVAC and cooling systems
- Open-set and closed-set fault detection and diagnostics in smart buildings
- Thermodynamic and transient performance analysis of VRF systems
- Waste heat recovery, organic Rankine cycles, and power-system repowering
- Building energy performance, uncertainty analysis, exergy, and techno-economic assessment

## Current Profile
